# Pathfinder 2e Character Sheet

A Bangle.js application to display your Pathfinder 2e character stats right on your wrist!

## Features
- **Overview**: See your HP, AC, Speed, Class, and Level.
- **Abilities**: View your Core Ability Scores and their Modifiers.
- **Defenses**: Quickly check your Saving Throws (Fortitude, Reflex, Will) and Perception.
- **Skills**: Check all of your skill modifiers.
- **Weapons**: Tap to view your attack bonuses and damage dice.
- **Equipment**: Check your current inventory.

## Setup
Your character data is bundled with the app in `data.json`.
